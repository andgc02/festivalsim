Help & Tutorial

Welcome to Festival Simulator!

  1. Start by hiring artists and vendors
  2. Launch marketing campaigns to attract attendees
  3. Manage your budget and reputation
  4. Handle dynamic events as they arise
  5. Advance time to see the results

Key mechanics to understand:

  • Budget Management: Balance spending between artists, vendors, and marketing
  • Reputation: Affects ticket sales and artist/vendor interest
  • Synergies: Hire related artists for bonus effects
  • Vendor Relationships: Some vendors work well together, others don't
  • Dynamic Events: Random events test your decision-making

Pro tips for festival success:

  • Mix popular and emerging artists for cost efficiency
  • Space out marketing campaigns for sustained impact
  • Use the layout designer to optimize crowd flow
  • Schedule popular artists at peak times
  • Keep emergency funds for unexpected events
  • Monitor artist and vendor satisfaction
